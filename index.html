Self-hosting NextCloud on Raspberry Pi

In the process of depending less on external cloud services, I wanted to self-host my personal 'cloud' to store my data. The best option to achieve that was to create a NextCloud instance on a Raspberry Pi. Having followed a few online tutorials, I wanted to describe some problems I've encountered during the setup, and the solutions I was able to come up with in order to solve them.
